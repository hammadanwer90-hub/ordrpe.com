Refund & Return Policy

Effective date: 25 April 2026

We aim to keep this fair and practical for customers. If there is an issue, contact support quickly so we can resolve it.

Eligible Cases

  • Wrong item delivered compared to the confirmed order.
  • Item arrives significantly damaged due to shipping/handling.
  • Authenticity concern on products sourced through us (requires review evidence).

Non-Eligible Cases

  • Change of mind after order is placed with supplier.
  • Size/fit mismatch when the selected size matches your requested size.
  • Minor packaging or batch-level visual differences from store photos.

Claim Window

Report issues within 48 hours of delivery with photos/video and order details on WhatsApp. Late claims may not be accepted.

Resolution Types

  • Full refund (where fault is verified and return/recovery is not possible).
  • Partial refund (for minor verified issues).
  • Replacement or re-order, depending on stock and case type.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days through the original payment method or agreed transfer method.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 April 2026

Ordrpe ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you order from us, request quotes, or contact us on WhatsApp/social media.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, phone number, city, delivery address.
  • Order details: product links, size preferences, quantity, and payment choice.
  • Communication data: messages sent to our WhatsApp/social pages.
  • Basic website analytics (if enabled) to understand traffic and improve the site.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide price quotes, place orders, and coordinate delivery.
  • To send updates about order status and support responses.
  • To prevent fraud and maintain transaction records.
  • To improve customer experience and service quality.

Sharing of Data

We only share required information with logistics/courier partners and payment service providers as needed to complete your order. We do not sell customer data.

Data Retention

We keep order and communication records for operational, accounting, and dispute-resolution purposes. You can request deletion of non-essential personal data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may ask to review, correct, or delete your personal information (where legally allowed). For requests, contact us on WhatsApp at +92 321 8705726.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our business evolves. The latest version is always published on this page.
